About Lily Be Clean

Hi, I’m Lily Be McCammon — founder of Lily Be Clean and passionate believer in the power of a tidy space. I’m a movie lover, a people person, and someone who truly values the comfort of home.

Lily Be Clean was born from a simple mission: to help people feel calm, refreshed, and recharged in their own homes. Based in St. George, Utah, I proudly serve the local community and surrounding areas — transforming homes and businesses into clean, peaceful spaces that support your well-being and lifestyle.
